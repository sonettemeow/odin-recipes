Kimchi Fried Rice

Kimchi Fried Rice

A Korean Staple Dish

A quick-to-cook go-to dish with simple ingredients, but full of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1/4 cup ground beef
  • 1 green onion, sliced, white and green parts separated
  • 1 cup kimchi, drained and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang or to taste (optional)
  • 3 cups cooked short-grain rice
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Heat canola oil in large skillet over high heat.
  2. Stir ground beef and white part of green onion and reduce heat to medium
  3. Cook and stir beef and green onion until meat is browned and onion is fragrant (1-2 minutes)
  4. Stir kimchi and gochujang into mixture and cook until warmed through and fragrant (2-4 minutes)
  5. Add rice; cook and stir until heated through and coated with gochujang (3-5 minutes)
  6. Drizzle sesame oil over mixture and stir to coat
  7. Garnish with green part of green onion
  8. Melt 1 teaspoon butter in skillet over medium-high heat
  9. Crack egg into pan and cook until white is completely set and yolk is thick (3-4 minutes)
  10. Place egg over fried rice
