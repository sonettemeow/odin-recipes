Cajun Chicken

A healthy, zesty, spicy, flavorful dish.

Served with rice and veggies or as a salad topping.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons dried Italian-style seasoning
  • garlic powder to taste
  • 2 tablespoons lemon pepper
  • 10 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded to 1/2 inch thickness

Steps:

  1. In large shallow dish, mix the following:
    • oil
    • Cajun seasoning
    • Italian seasoning
    • garlic powder
    • lemon pepper
  2. Place chicken in mixture and turn to coat
  3. Cover and refrigerate for 1/2 hour
  4. Preheat grill to high heat
  5. Lightly oil grill; drain chicken and; discard marinade
  6. Place chicken on hot grill and cook for 6-8 minutes on each side or until juices run clear
